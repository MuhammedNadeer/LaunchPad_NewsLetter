Introduction

One of the most prestigious event of IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn that is determined to serve the goal of creating opportunities for engineering students and graduates.

Launchpad Kerala 2024 is a premier job fair that brings together talented individuals and innovative companies in the technical and engineering fields. This collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn aims to create meaningful connections that drive progress and innovation in Kerala's job market.

Call to Action

Ready to launch your career to new heights? Don't miss out on the incredible opportunities awaiting you at Launchpad Kerala 2024! Join us to:

  • Network with Industry Leaders: Forge connections with professionals and companies paving the way in innovation. Build relationships that could lead to internships, co-op opportunities, and future employment.
  • Gain Insider Insights: Get a firsthand look at the latest trends, technologies, and challenges shaping your industry. Discover what it takes to succeed from experts in the field.
  • Exposure to Diverse Career Paths: Open your mind to a world of possibilities across various sectors and industries. Find the perfect fit for your interests and skills among our diverse lineup of companies.
  • Fuel Your Personal Growth: Embrace the journey of self-discovery and development. Push your limits, seize new opportunities, and emerge stronger, more confident, and ready to conquer your career goals.
